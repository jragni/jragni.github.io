About Me

I'm Jhensen Ray Agni Mechanical Engineer turned Software Engineer.

Los Angeles native, with a passion for leading highly effective engineering teams and changing the world one line of code at a time.

I am currently building and testing front end features to create an intuitive staff and customer experience at Rithm School, where I was also a graduate of their Full Stack Software Engineering Bootcamp.
